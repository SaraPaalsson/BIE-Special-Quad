Welcome to toolbox_project's documentation!
===========================================

Solving Laplace's equation with a boundary integral method and special quadrature for near singular integrals. Based on method developed by   `Ojala and Tornberg <http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0021999115003812>`_ .
